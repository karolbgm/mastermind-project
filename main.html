Instructions:

The castle hides a powerful and coveted magical sphere deep within its walls. Legends say that this sphere possesses unimaginable powers. Only the cleverest code breakers can reveal the code guarding this mystical artifact.
  • Objective: Break the castle's code within 10 turns to unveil the magical sphere.
  • Gameplay:
    1. Click on a color peg to choose a color.
    2. Click on the desired position in the check row to place it.
    3. Receive feedback from the castle:
      • A black keypeg: Correct color, correct position.
      • A white key peg: Correct color, wrong position.
      • No key peg: Wrong color.
    4. Continue to guess and adapt your strategy to uncover the code.
